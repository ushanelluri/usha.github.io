Project Title: Multi-Source Sentiment-Driven Stock Prediction and Trading Strategy

This project aimed to enhance stock market prediction by incorporating sentiment data from financial news, social media, and reports. I contributed to the sentiment analysis module (MSS 1.2), where I trained and validated models using lexicon-based methods, supervised machine learning, and deep learning (LSTM). I also worked on feature engineering and helped evaluate the strategy through backtesting and simulated forward testing. I collaborated with other team members responsible for data ingestion and trading rule implementation.

Project Overview

The system integrates sentiment data from multiple sources and processes it to generate trading signals. These signals are combined with market indicators and evaluated across historical and simulated environments.
